Rainy Night

A minimalist sleep app for iPhone

Rainy Night is a minimalist sleep app with relaxing sounds like rain, a stream, and brown noise. It includes a timer to quiet the sounds, and Night Watch.

Night Watch mimics the smoothing lights of a television by cycling random colors at random intervals. Night Watch can automatically dim your screen when it starts.

Rainy Night is not designed to cure or relieve sleep disorders. If you have trouble sleeping or don't feel well rested, please speak to your doctor.
