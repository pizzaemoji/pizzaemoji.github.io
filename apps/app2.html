Rainy Night

A minimalist sleep app for iPhone

Rainy Night is a minimalist sleep app with relaxing sounds like rain, a small stream, a cozy fire, brown noise, and more, with a sleep timer and Night Watch.

Night Watch mimics the soothing lights of a television. Night Watch can automatically dim your screen for the perfect soft glow — and it pairs great with your bedside charging stand.

Rainy Night is not designed to cure or relieve sleep disorders. If you have trouble sleeping or don't feel well rested, please speak to your doctor.
