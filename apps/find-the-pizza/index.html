Find the Pizza

A silly game with emojis

Find the Pizza is a silly little game made in an afternoon with a bunch of emojis. It’s 100% free on the App Store.

One of those silly emojis is hiding the 🍕! Watch carefully, then tap the emoji when you find it. If you tap the wrong emoji, you’ll lose a 💛. Pretty easy, right? Be quick — there’s a 🚨 on level 25… Keep playing until it’s game over!

Did I mention it’s free? 😂
