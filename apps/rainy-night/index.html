Rainy Night

A minimalist sleep app for iPhone

Rainy Night is a minimalist sleep app for iPhone with relaxing sounds, a sleep timer, and Night Watch.

Night Watch mimics the soothing lights of a television as you listen to the sounds of a cool rain, a lazy stream, a cozy fire, and more. Night Watch can automatically dim your screen for the perfect soft glow, and it pairs great with your favorite bedside charging stand.

Completely free with no ads and no tracking on the App Store.
