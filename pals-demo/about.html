About Pals

Pals is built for humans, by humans.

Most apps are designed to be "infinite," fueled by algorithms that feed on your emotions. We think that's exhausting. We intentionally left out the influencers, AI, and ads so there's more room for your life.

Real-World Connections: There are no discovery tabs or suggested friends; if you want to find someone, you have to know them in the real world first.

Fade Away: Because relationships have seasons, our app is the only one that allows silent conversations to naturally fade away.

Privacy without Profit: We aren't here to collect and sell your data; our technology ensures that even the humans who build the app can’t see your names, messages, or photos.

We're glad you're here.
