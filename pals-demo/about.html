The Pals Design
Philosophy

Pals is built for humans, by humans.

Most apps are designed to be "infinite," fueled by algorithms that know you better than your friends do. We think that's exhausting. We intentionally left out the influencers, AI, and ads so there's more room for your actual life.

Real-World Connections: There are no discovery tabs or suggested friends; if you want to find someone, you have to know them in the real world first.

Mindful Sharing: Message your friends as much as you want, but we limit sharing your Moments to ensure that what you see is meaningful.

Fade Away: Because relationships have seasons, our app is the only one that allows silent conversations to naturally fade away.

We're glad you're here.
