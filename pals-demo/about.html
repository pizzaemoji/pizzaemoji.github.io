About Pals

Pals is built for humans, by humans.

Pals is built on the belief that social media can be safe and fun. We aren't driven by chasing profits and disrupting the world — we're here to try and make the internet a little better. Here's how Pals does that:

Real-world connections: Pals is designed for the people you know in the real world. You can be yourself in a safe space. There are no FYPs or suggested friends, and nothing is ever shared publicly. We think the internet is better this way.

It's okay to take a break: We hope you enjoy Pals, but we also hope you enjoy real life even more. It's okay to put us down and go outside. You won't be missing out on anything.

No AI or algorithms: There are friend or follower suggestions because Pals doesn't use algorithms to generate content or keep you addicted. Pals doesn't generate AI slop, either. We prefer humans connecting with other real humans.

Privacy without profit:: We aren't here to collect and sell your data; our technology ensures that even the humans who build the app can't see your name, messages, or photos.

We care about you, and making the best app for you: Don't believe us because we said it, believe it because we've earned it. Make us earn your trust, and hold us accountable when we mess up. We'll make the best app we can make, and we hope you enjoy the experience.

We're glad you're here.
