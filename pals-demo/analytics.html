Helping Pals Work Better

Data Analytics in Plain English

What is this?

This makes Pals better for you and your Pals. Most apps follow you around the internet to see what you like so they can sell you stuff. Pals doesn't do that. We only look at "The Big Numbers" to make sure the app is running smoothly for everyone.

How does it work?

This is an optional setting that is off by default. If you turn this on, your app sends us a few numbers every day. It’s like a digital headcount. We see that someone sent 10 messages today, but we have no idea that "someone" was you. No names, no messages, no photos—just numbers.

Why do we want these numbers?

We use these numbers to fix bugs and make the app work better. We do not sell or share this data with anyone.

Data Transparency

We believe you should know exactly what numbers are leaving your device. All data is sent anonymously in numerical form.

Daily App Opens Reason: To ensure our servers can handle the "Morning Rush."
Messages Sent & Received Reason: To monitor network load and delivery reliability.
Photo Activity Reason: To understand storage needs for encrypted media.
Connection Health Reason: Tracking active vs. disconnected Pals helps us refine the "Natural Fade" algorithm.
