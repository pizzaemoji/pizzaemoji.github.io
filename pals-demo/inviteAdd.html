How to add or invite a Pal

If you're inviting a person to join Pals, ask them to tap Accept an Invite from the welcome screen. Then tap your phones lightly together.

If they're already on Pals, ask them to open the Add or Invite page from the Profile Menu. Then tap your phones lightly together.

Or, they can snap a picture of your QR code, or you can send them a link. (But we think tapping is more fun.)
