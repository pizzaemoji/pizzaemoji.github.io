Pals Philosophy
and Privacy

This document was written by humans, for humans. Please read it to see why Pals isn't like the apps you've used before.

Mindful Design

Most apps are greedy for your attention. We think that's kind of cringe.

  • Live Your Life: It's okay to put us down. You can go outside or read a book. Your messages aren't going anywhere.
  • Quality over Quantity: You can message your Pals as much as you want, but Moments are limited to three per day. This keeps the feed meaningful.
  • Quiet by Default: We don't obsessively vibrate your phone while you're away. We just assume you're off doing awesome things.

No Spying, No Selling

We do not spy on you, sell your data, or treat you like the product.

  • End-to-End Encryption (E2EE): Your messages and photos are encrypted: only you and your Pals can see them.
  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Our server delivers your messages and then immediately forgets them. We don't know what you said.
  • You Own Your Life: Your photos and messages belong to you. We have no right to use your life to train AI or sell ads.

The Natural Fade

Relationships change, and your digital footprint should too.

  • 182-Day Fade: If you haven't messaged your Pal in 6 months, the connection dims. Moments access is revoked until you reconnect.
  • The 1-Year Rule: Pals is a reflection of who you are now. We automatically remove messages and media older than 365 days.
  • Honest Deletion: If you leave, your profile leaves with you. We keep your account in a "ghost state" for 72 hours, then we purge your number forever.

Control Your Space

You have the tools to manage your circle without the drama:

  • Silence: Stop the notifications, keep the friend.
  • Hide: Remove a Pal from view until you're ready to talk again.
  • End Chat: A polite closure. It closes the connection but keeps the chat history.
  • Block: The nuclear option for those who don't take the hint.

Our Promise

No Spying. No Selling. No Bots. We are humans, and we promise to treat you with kindness and respect.
