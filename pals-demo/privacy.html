Pals Privacy

in Plain English

This document was written by humans, for humans. Please read it to see why Pals isn't like the apps you've used before.

Mindful Design

Most apps are greedy for your attention. We think that's disrespectful.

Live Your Life: It's okay to put us down. You can go outside or read a book. Your messages aren't going anywhere.

No Stranger Danger: Pals is designed for the people you know in the real world. You can be yourself in a safe space. No strangers, no randos, no one sliding into your DMs uninvited.

Quiet by Default: We don't obsessively vibrate your phone when you're not using it. We just assume you're off doing awesome things.

No Spying, No Selling

We do not spy on you, sell your data, or treat you like the product.

End-to-End Encryption: Your messages and photos are encrypted. Only you and your Pals can see them.

Zero-Knowledge Design: Our server delivers your messages and forgets them. There's nothing to store. Nothing to hack. Nothing to sell.

Your Messages Are Yours: Your conversations live on your device. We don't think anyone should own your conversations except you.

You Aren't the Product: Because we don't answer to advertisers, we can focus on building the best product for you. And the product is Pals.

Built for Real Life

Relationships change, and your digital footprint should too.

The Natural Fade: Pals isn't an archive of your life, it's a moment in time. Old messages and photos naturally fade away, so you can focus on today.

Private by Default: Pals doesn't have public pages or discover feeds. Nothing is shared publicly. We think the internet is better this way.

Honest Deletion: If you leave, your profile leaves with you. It's really that simple.

Our Promise

We are humans, and we promise to treat you like a human, too, with kindness and respect.

We're glad you're here.
