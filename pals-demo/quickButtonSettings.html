Quick Button Settings

Select Quick Buttons

Choose up to four buttons to display on the home screen. (This setting has no effect in the demo.)

Arrange Quick Buttons

Drag and drop the buttons below to customize the order of the buttons. (This does not work in the web demo.)

Keep Buttons on Left

The buttons are positioned by default on the bottom right of the app window. Turn this setting on to move them to the left. This is best for people who hold their phone primarily with their left hand.
