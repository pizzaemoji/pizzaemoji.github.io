Quick Button Settings

Select Quick Buttons

Choose up to four buttons to display on the home screen. (This setting has no effect in the demo.)

Arrange Quick Buttons

Drag and drop the buttons below to customize their order. (This does not work in the demo.)

Button Position

Keep Buttons on Left

The buttons are positioned by default on the bottom right of the screen. Turn this on to move them to the bottom left side. (This setting has no effect in the demo.)
