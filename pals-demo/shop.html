Pals Shop

Welcome to the (future home of the) Pals Digital Shop!

Let’s talk about what it means to be free on the internet. The reason most apps are “free” is because they’re selling you. They track your location, read your messages, and map out your friendships just to serve you a “personalized” ad. To us, that’s not a social network, it’s a digital billboard that follows you into your private life.

The modern social internet is kind of a mess right now: You can’t scroll for ten seconds without an algorithm trying to sell you a life-changing supplement or a bot trying to lure you into a crypto scam. How is that social?

When we made Pals, we wanted a place without ads, influencers, and brands. Just a place to be with the people we actually know.

But for Pals to succed, we need it to be a sustainable community. Enter: The Pals Shop. The shop is our way of keeping the app independent and ad-free. It’s a direct exchange: users get cool ways to express themselves, and we get to keep the lights on without ever having to answer to an advertiser.

We believe that Pals can succeed on this model. No loot boxes or gambling, just digital goods—wallpapers, chat effects, and profile flare—that make a digital space feel unique.

And here’s the most important part: It’s all optional. Texting friends and sharing moments from our lives will always be free. The shop is for the people who want to support an ad-free future.
