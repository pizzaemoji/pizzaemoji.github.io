What's new?

Everything! Pals is a new kind of social messaging app, and while there's no messaging backend at this time, the app's UI and design ideas are starting to form.

The idea behind Pals is privacy and simplicity. Social media is a mess, and I think most people are rightfully hesitant to share on the major social media platforms.

But what if there was a social messaging platform that treated users like humans and wasn't out to profit with harmful designs and addictive algorithms?
