Letter Spacing & Line Height

leading-relaxed: This is the standard for blog posts or long-form reading. It gives the lines room to breathe so the eye doesn't get lost.

leading-tight: This is for headlines. It keeps the words grouped together as a single visual unit, preventing the "falling apart" look.

tracking-widest: Great for small, authoritative labels.
